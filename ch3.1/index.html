Covalent Bonding

Covalent bonds are formed when non-metal atoms from the right hand side of the periodic table share pairs of electrons in an attempt to achieve complete or stable valence shells.

Summary:

  • Takes place between non-metal atoms.
  • Sharing pairs of electrons between atoms.
  • From small molecules to giant structures.
  • Form 3-dimensional shapes to maximize electron pair separation.
  • Non-bonding and bonding pairs of electrons have differing repulsion effects.
