Bonding

There are around 100 different elements but there are countless compounds on Earth. These compounds occur when atoms combine in different ways by bonding. Atoms bond to achieve stable electron arrangements and they bond in different ways. Atoms come together in small groups or large lattices and structures, with similar types of atoms or very different atoms. The properties of the bonded atoms are very different to the raw elements.

The experiences on these bonding pages will help you explore the way valence electrons on different atoms interact and form a variety of covalent and ionic compounds. One key feature that these experiences provide is the chance to explore the 3D shape of the molecules. Be sure to spin them around and look from multiple angles.
